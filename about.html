About this site

I am not an expert in anything. I am a recent coding bootcamp graduate. My purpose in creating this site can best be characterized via a quote from on of the most moving works in Buddhist literature, How to Lead an Awakened Life by Santideva.

There is nothing here that has not been said before, Nor do I have any skill in composition. Thus, I have no concern for others and I have composed my text solely to cultivate my own mind. Since my virtue is cultivateed, My faith thereby increases in power. Nonetheless, if someone else with an outlook like my own Sees this, it would be meaningful.

Thus, my purpose is to solidify my own knowledge of this practice. My hope is that the meadium of a blog will allow others to benefit from my 'writing to learn' endeavor.
